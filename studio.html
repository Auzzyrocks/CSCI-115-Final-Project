Located in the scenic Harewood neighbourhood of Nanaimo, BC, the surrounding Vancouver Island wilderness provides a scerene and insirational backdrop to recording your music.

Echo-7 features the capacity to record a full-band simultaneously, and offers a wide variety of instruments to help bring inspiration to your projects.

From guitars to synthesizers, and even a electronic drum kit, there is amble equipment to bring your songs to life.

After achieiving a Diploma of Digital Audio Production from The Southern Alberta Institute of Technology in 2016, Austin Shouli founded Echo-7 Recording Studios, and began recording for local Calgary artists in his home studio.

Our list of equipment includes:

  • Tascam DM 3200 Hybrid Recording Console
  • Avalon 747-SP Mix Bus Processor
  • Adam A8X Studio Monitors
