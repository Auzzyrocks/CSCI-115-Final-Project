Austin is the founder and sole-proprietor of Echo 7 Studios. Specializing in Audio Production, Marketing, and Communications, Austin has helped artists and small buisness's across Western Canada to realize their projects big and small.

Austin holds a certificate in Digital Audio Production from The Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT), and a Diploma of Journalism from SAIT. After receving his education, Austin went on to work for Live Nation Canada as a Production Assistant.



Working in Calgary's largest venues, Austin gained valuable experience working with artists such as Lynryd Skynyrd, Slayer, and Billy Idol. Festival's such as Chaos, Oxford Stomp / Roundup, and Chasing Summer were exciting and offer valuable insight into running large scale events. When the global pandemic of 2020 began, Austin shifted his talents back towards Marking, Communications, and Audio Production. Constructing, and then working out of his home studio, Austin worked with local Calgary musicians and buisness. In April of 2021 Austin relocated to Nanaimo, BC to focus on recording and to pursue his latest passion - computer science.
